Biography

Scherazade is a junior web developer with a passion for creating user-friendly websites. After earning a qualification from MEST-Generation partnership, Scherazade gained practical experience through an internship at a local startup. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Scherazade focuses on front-end.

In addition to coding, Scherazade enjoys contributing to open-source projects and staying current with industry trends. Outside of work, Scherazade loves swimming and reading. With a commitment to continuous learning, Scherazade is excited to grow in the tech field and tackle new challenges.
